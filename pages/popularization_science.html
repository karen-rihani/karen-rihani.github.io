“Expérimentarium” Network

“Expérimentarium” is carried in four regions of France: Bourgogne, Franche-comté, Normandie and Paca, by the unit of scientific culture. This network opened a space of meeting between young researchers and pupils of primary classes, middle school and the general public. The objective is to expose the researcher's job to the society. To learn more, visit their website.

My thesis in 180 seconds 2018 (MT180)

My thesis in 180 seconds allows PhD students to present their research topic, in French and in simple terms, to a wide and diverse audience. Each student must give, in three minutes, a clear, concise and convincing presentation on his research project. All with the support of a single slide. To learn more, visit their website.
